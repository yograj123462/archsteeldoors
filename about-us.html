Our Work Process

Each of the products we model is of the finest raw materials, and quality checked before it reaches you. Our custom style designs assembled according to the industry standards. Whatever you choose from our catalog or order a custom design, each product has unique characteristics to create a remarkable visual appeal and blend with your interiors/exteriors.

When you contact our team, we will ask you some preliminary project details and be happy to answer any queries you have. Next, we will assign you a dedicated project manager who will be responsible for your project. The project manager will act as a single point of contact in case you have any questions during the project’s completion. The project manager will ask you what you are looking for, your preferences, location, and size estimate to give you a realistic quote and suggest the best products based on your climatic conditions.

Exact measurements taken after the quote is approved. Measures may require us to remove the trim from around the door to verify the rough opening size. At this point, we will move ahead to create some initial drawings. Once we get your approval on them, the manufacturing and assembling part starts. Lastly, we are ready for the final testing and installation.
